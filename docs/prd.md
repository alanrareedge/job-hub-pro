# Job Hub Pro™

## Vision

A complete operating system for UK trades businesses.

The platform should help businesses:

- Win more work
- Reduce administration
- Improve cash flow
- Improve communication
- Manage jobs and teams
- Improve customer experience
- Increase repeat business

## Users

- Business Owner
- Office Administrator
- Surveyor
- Operative
- Subcontractor
- Customer

## Core Modules

1. Customers
2. Properties
3. Jobs
4. Scheduling
5. Teams
6. Materials
7. Suppliers
8. Financials
9. Documents
10. Reporting
11. Customer Portal

## Integrations

- Go High Level
- Stripe
- Xero (future)
- Twilio (future)

## Principles

- Mobile first
- Simple to use
- Fast
- Built specifically for trades
- Complete audit trail
